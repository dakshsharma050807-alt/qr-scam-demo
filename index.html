QR Code Safety Awareness

Learn how scammers use fake QR codes to trick people into visiting harmful websites.

⚠ CYBERSECURITY DEMONSTRATION

You scanned a QR code without knowing where it would lead.


Scammers often use rewards, discounts, and urgency to attract clicks.


Always verify before scanning.

How to Stay Safe

  • Check where the QR code came from before scanning it.
  • Preview the link and look for misspellings or unusual website names.
  • Be cautious of messages promising prizes, discounts, or urgent action.
  • Do not enter passwords, bank details, or personal information on unknown pages.<
